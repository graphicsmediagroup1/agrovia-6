Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Agrovia

What industries does Agrovia support?▼
Agrovia supports ten industry verticals: Agriculture, Oil & Gas, Retail, Construction, Manufacturing, Professional Services, Real Estate, Healthcare, Restaurants, and Trucking. Each gets its own chart of accounts, IRS tools, and compliance calculators.
How does the AI Copilot work?▼
The AI Copilot is powered by Claude and analyzes your firm's financial data in real-time. Ask questions in plain English like "which client has the highest margin?" or "flag any unusual transactions this month" and get instant, accurate answers.
Is my data secure?▼
Absolutely. Agrovia uses Supabase with row-level security, ensuring each firm only sees their own data. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit with 256-bit encryption. We follow SOC 2 compliance standards.
Can I switch plans anytime?▼
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the billing settings inside the CRM. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated. Cancel anytime with no penalties.
What export formats are supported?▼
Agrovia exports to PDF (professional, branded reports), Excel (.xlsx with full formatting), and CSV. Industry-specific reports like Schedule F and WIP schedules are generated automatically with one click.
Do you offer a free trial?▼
Yes — every new account starts with a 7-day free trial that includes all features, including the AI Copilot. No credit card required. After 7 days, choose Starter ($49/mo) or Professional ($99/mo) to continue.
